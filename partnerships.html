Our Industry Collaboration Approach

The Department of Information & Communication Technology at MUT maintains strong partnerships with leading technology companies and organizations. These collaborations ensure our curriculum remains relevant to industry needs and provide valuable opportunities for our students and faculty.

Through these partnerships, we offer internships, collaborative research projects, guest lectures from industry experts, and access to cutting-edge technologies.

Microsoft Innovation Lab

Established in 2022 through our partnership with Microsoft, this lab provides students with access to cutting-edge development tools and Azure cloud resources.

  • Over 200 students trained on Azure technologies annually
  • 15 student projects deployed to production environments
  • 3 startups launched from student projects
